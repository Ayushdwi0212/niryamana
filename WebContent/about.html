An unchanging world

There are times when we notice small things which we can fix by ourselves, but we leave it to others to do the job. Unattended, the problem which was small yesterday becomes a huge nuisance today. Litter in the streets, clogged drainways, there are so many things we can fix ourselves, but we choose otherwise, only to complaint about it later.

Hoping for a change

Niryamana intends to be the platform where people can come together to find ways to solve these everyday problems. And the best part, they do so voluntarily, without expecting anything in return. Okay, maybe a little thank you won't hurt anyone, but other than that, there's no payment involved for the volunteers.

Our sole motivation

We realize that it's an ambitious project, and the world we usually portray is not a place where such an idea can flourish. However, we believe in one of the core traits of human beings; humanity. It's something we all have, and we are counting on it to bring about small changes in the world we live in. The possibilities are endless; it's up to you to see where we go from here. Welcome to Niryamana. We hope you find what you were looking for.

How did this even began?

There's a joke in Sicily, that the world is too cruel for a child to have just a single father. Niryamana is the child of five fathers, and each of us contribute to the seriousness of the joke. Niryamana started as an idea of a teenager, still green in the ways of the world. Soon though, it took ahold of four college students, who were looking for an idea for their college project. This unlikely collaboration ended up creating a web portal, with a singular aim to help. Even the name, Niryamana, comes from Sanskrit, and means assistance.

Niryamana is a non-profit initiative to connect people together in hopes of making Indian communities better. To know more about our mission and lend your support, do check out our About page and our social media pages.
