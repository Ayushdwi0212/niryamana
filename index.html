Volunteer for your people, your community, for yourself

The idea of volunteering has taken on a completely different form than what was its originally envisioned. The modern volunteer works for an organization to help people of a specific group.

While there's nothing wrong with the idea itself, there exists an unaddressed fundamental problem; the fundamentals. The grassroots, which nurture the social behemoth we live in, are left untended because of the focus on the niche.

Niryamana is a non-profit initiative to connect people together in hopes of making Indian communities better. To know more about our mission and lend your support, do check out our About page and our social media pages.
